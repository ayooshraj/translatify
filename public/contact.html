üå± How Translatify Started

Our idea for Translatify was sparked during late-night brainstorming in our 4th semester. We saw a real need for smarter, more accessible text translation‚Äîespecially for websites and digital tools targeting global audiences. With ChatGPT, Google Translate, and DeepL being amazing yet limited for scale or regional tone, we wanted to build something that starts small but has big potential.

So, we built a **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** using frontend tech, API integration, and custom backend to allow users to convert text into over 249+ languages. We added a pricing model for scale. This project not only taught us collaboration and full stack architecture, but it also gave us a glimpse into solving real-world SaaS problems.
